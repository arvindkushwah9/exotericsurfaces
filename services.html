Facade Solutions in Delhi NCR & Noida

Exoteric Surfaces specializes in facade solutions across Delhi NCR, Noida, Greater Noida, and Gurugram. From ACP cladding to structural glazing, we deliver modern, durable, and aesthetic exteriors.

Facade Solutions

We provide turnkey facade design and installation services including aluminium composite panels, Fundermax cladding, stone cladding and glass facades. Our team ensures energy efficiency, durability and aesthetics.

Glazing Systems

Our glazing solutions include curtain walls, structural glazing, partition glazing and skylights. Designed for transparency and structural integrity, we deliver modern designs with top safety standards.

Porcelain & Thin Tiles

Exquisite porcelain tiles and thin slabs in sizes 600×1200mm, 1000×3000×3mm and 1200×2400×6mm. Perfect for floors, walls and facades with low maintenance and long life.

Louvers

Exterior and interior louvers in aluminium, WPC, ACP and other finishes. Adds functionality and style to building exteriors while enhancing ventilation and sun control.

Railings

Premium glass railings, stainless steel and mild steel railings. Options include straight line glass railing, tilted glass railing and customizable combinations.

Fabrication

We offer in-house fabrication for MS, aluminium, stainless steel and structural steel works. Custom-built to suit your project requirements with precision and durability.
