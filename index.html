About Us

We, Exoteric Surfaces, are the perfect end for customers who look for choosing the finest selection of wall and floor tiles. Our tagline is “TRANSFORMING SPACES TO PERFECTION”. We strive to give the best materials at competitive prices along with a pleasant experience and unmatched after-sales service.

We endure gratification for every experience that we offer, created to share something truly meaningful. Perfection doesn’t exist, but we promise you a better product experience.

Our Philosophy

We focus on delivering awe-inspiring products with high-end finishes which is clearly evident from our 3Cs Design Philosophy: Contrive, Create, Customize. This philosophy forms the building block of EXOTERIC SURFACES' brand positioning which motivates us to deliver aesthetic and functional value to customers of this new and futuristic millennium.

Who We Are

We are a full-service building & facade consultant providing turnkey solutions for facade, structural glazing, curtain wall, aluminium doors & windows, railings, tiles, MS/SS framework and many more solutions.

What We Do

For the retail and building industries, we provide development and installation of structural glazing systems, thin tile building cladding, glass facades, aluminium doors & windows, and complete turnkey facade solutions.

Turnkey Facade Solutions

A turnkey project is designed, developed and equipped with all facilities by a company under contract, handed over to the buyer when ready to operate. Our company provides complete turnkey solutions at agreed costs and timelines while ensuring quality and performance.

Porcelain Thin Tiles

Louvers

Railings

Glass railings, SS and MS railings — straight line glass railing, tilted glass railing and various combinations.

Main Entrance Doors & Gates

High-quality entrance doors & gates: aluminium louver profile gates, SS gates, MS gates with HPL/wood/ACP finishes.

Aluminium & uPVC Windows

Jaalis

Decorative perforated screens in aluminium, SS, stone and more — used for architectural decoration and interior enhancement.

Glazing System

Structurally glazed systems and curtain wall assemblies that create seamless transparency with fewer interruptions from metal framing.

Cladding System

Aluminium composite panel (ACP) cladding, Fundermax/HPL, stone cladding, dry cladding systems and certified panel installers.

Fabrication

Custom fabrication services for MS, aluminium, stainless steel and structural steel to meet project-specific needs.

Flooring

Floor covering solutions for various needs — laminate and hardwood floorings among our offerings. Floor covering describes any finish material applied over a floor structure to provide a walking surface.
